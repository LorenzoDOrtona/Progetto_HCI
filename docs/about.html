Our mission is to solve overcrowding with smart, efficient solutions.

ZoneFlow is an innovative service designed to reduce overcrowding by directing people to less crowded areas. Using advanced algorithms, it provides real-time recommendations for optimal movement, improving crowd flow and enhancing overall efficiency in public spaces and events.
